    Project 2 (2024)

    Carved a 12x12 block of mounted and unmounted linoleum. I gessored the area that was carved to help make cleaning off the ink easier. I inked up the linoleum using oil based printing inks and a roller. After applying the ink, I rolled it through a printing press.
